LOCAL ORDERS

Delivery Time:

We at SR garments aim to process and dispatch your order within 24 hours once the order is confirmed. You are requested to be available on the provided contact number so our team can reach you.

Please note that if the item(s) on your order are unfit for dispatch due to quality or any other reason, they will not be shipped or will be shipped partially. You will be informed via phone or email in case a shipment is delayed due to any given reason.

COD orders are verified via telephone or email before dispatching. However, in case of repeat customers, Clearance sale and other promotions orders may be processed without telephonic verification.

The estimated average delivery time across Pakistan is 2 to 4 working days (Please note that during sale delivery time may exceed due to high volume order log)

Delivery Charges:

We offer free Delivery for all the orders above Rs. 1500/- . However, if your order is below Rs. 1500/- then standard delivery rates will be charged based on the payment method:

Cash on delivery: Rupees 350/-.

Online/Bank Transfer/Easy pay: Rupees 100/-.

INTERNATIONAL ORDERS

Delivery Time:

The estimated delivery time for international order is 3 to 7 working days after the order has been dispatched.

The tracking number you receive from us in an Order Status email can be used to track the order on DHL’s website (www.DHL.com).

Delivery charges:

Charges for all international delivery vary from country to country depending upon city, state, country and locality. To calculate shipment charges you can select any product and checkout with your delivery address to calculate the estimated delivery charges (doesn’t include any taxes & duties).

Taxes & Duties:

Please note that international orders may be subject to taxes & duties and will be responsibility of the customer. Any applicable duties or fees will be collected at the time of order delivery.
