welcome to SR Shop

SR Curtas is a fully integrated Knitted garments manufacturing and export company with latest machinery. SR Curtas is the Vertical setup in Pakistan manufacturing Knitted Fashion/Basic garments for European customers. At SR Curtas, We offer our clients the finest products possible then enhance this craftsmanship with the added benefit of some of the world's most competitive labor costs. When it comes to style quality and fit, SR Curtas Product's can hold their own against merchandise manufactured anywhere else in the world. And when it comes to cost, no one else can even come close to our values. The management believes in the theory of Continuous Improvement, and is always looking for ways to improve the quality of production in the light of modern quality concepts. SR Curtas has not stopped its struggle for excellence and the management is committed to provide world-class quality products to its clients all over the world. The sole object of the company is; To operate a tailoring and stitching plant for manufacturing of ready made garments from cloth manufactured in Pakistan, of various types, designs and patterns and to market and the same in and outside Pakistan. To operate an export oriented unit for manufacturing of Men's, women's and kids fashion garments.
